Our Vision

When it comes to equity, you can't just wing it

Nail the product. Get new investors. Make key hires. Grow like crazy. As a founder, these are your only goals.
Whatever helps you get there – great. Everything else just gets in the way. Same goes for managing equity.

The new way founders
do equity

That’s why we built Altshare – the only equity management platform made just for entrepreneurs.

Altshare is based on decades of experience as a leading Israeli provider of trustee, stock administration, compensation plans and administration services and trusted by 2,000 companies and organizations, over 100 VC firms, and 60k+ beneficiaries worldwide.
